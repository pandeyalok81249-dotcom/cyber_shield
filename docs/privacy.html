Privacy Policy for Cyber Shield

Effective Date: 10 July 2026

Cyber Shield is a fraud and scam protection app designed to help users detect suspicious links, scam messages, phishing patterns, and report fraud-related activity.

Information We Collect

  • Email address for login and account management.
  • User ID generated by Firebase Authentication.
  • Scan history, including links or messages manually entered by the user.
  • Fraud reports submitted by the user, such as suspicious phone numbers, emails, UPI IDs, websites, and report details.
  • Basic app activity needed to provide app functionality and security features.

Information We Do Not Collect

Cyber Shield does not secretly read SMS, call logs, contacts, photos, files, location, microphone, or camera data. Users manually paste messages or links for scanning.

How We Use Information

  • User authentication and account management.
  • Saving scan history for the user.
  • Saving fraud reports submitted by the user.
  • Improving fraud and scam protection features.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and compliance.

Data Storage and Security

Cyber Shield uses Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to store account data, scan history, and fraud reports. Data is transmitted using secure connections.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be processed by Firebase/Google Cloud only as a service provider for authentication, storage, and app functionality.

User Control and Deletion

Users can clear scan history, clear fraud reports, and delete their account from the app settings.

Disclaimer

Cyber Shield provides fraud and scam detection assistance based on security patterns. It does not guarantee complete protection from all scams. Users should always verify links, messages, callers, and payment requests before sharing OTPs, passwords, PINs, or financial information.
